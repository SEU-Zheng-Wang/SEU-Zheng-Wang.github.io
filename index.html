    Research Interest

      Zheng Wang (Senior Member, IEEE) received the B.S. degree in electronic and information engineering from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Nanjing, China, in 2009, and the M.S. degree in communications from University of Manchester, Manchester, U.K., in 2010. He received the Ph.D. degree in communication engineering from Imperial College London, UK, in 2015. Since 2021, he has been an Associate Professor in the School of Information and Engineering, Southeast University, Nanjing, China. From 2015 to 2016 he served as a Research Associate at Imperial College London, UK. From 2016 to 2017 he was an senior engineer with Radio Access Network R&D division, Huawei Technologies Co.. From 2017 to 2021 he was an Lecture & Associate Professor at the College of Electronic and Information Engineering, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), Nanjing, China.

      With respect to the Physical Layer of Wireless Communications, his current research interests can be summarized as:
      Low-complexity, distributed, intelligent MIMO Signal Processing for Massive MIMO, Cell-Free MIMO, XL-MIMO, ISAC-MIMO, H-MIMO, RIS-MIMO......
      专注于提供低复杂度、分布式、智能化的高维信号处理解决方案，服务于各种MIMO相关场景
